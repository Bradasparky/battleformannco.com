# Rules

## Server Rules
### Exploits
- Do not exploit commands, gameplay features, or map spots
- If it feels like an exploit, it probably is
- Exploits should be reported to developers via Discord https://discord.gg/EeGxbzmbAm
### Chat/Mic Spam
- Keep chat binds to a minimum
- No repeated unintelligible gibberish clogging up chat
- Mic spam of any kind is not allowed
### Chat Ettiquette
- Read the room before you speak
- Do not discuss topics that are sexual in nature
- Do not be intentionally hostile to illicit negative reactions
- Do not bait people into breaking rules
- Do not discuss politics
### Harassment & Discrimination
Harrassment is defined as:
- Unwanted actions towards another person meant to provoke,or make them feel unwelcome or uncomfortable
- Making repeated unwanted actions towards another person despite the content being light hearted in nature
- This applies whether done by an individual or a group of people
- Do not discriminate against players based on race, religion, ethnicity, location of origin, gender, or sexual orientation
### Illegal/NSFW Content
- **DO NOT**
- **THANK YOU**
### UGC Content
- Sprays are disabled
## VSH Gameplay Rules
Friendlying is not allowed and is defined as:
- Refusing to participate in the objective
- Refusing to kill other players who aren't participating in the objective

Stalling is not allowed and is defined as:
- Actions that halt the game state from progressing
- Dragging on rounds when victory is trivial
